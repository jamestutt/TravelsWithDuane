Dion and Duane
26th Aug 2007 - 30th Aug 2007
The end days!

With time in NZ drawing to a close it was time to assess what there was left to do. One more LOTR film and an epic voyage from Wellington to Christchurch. So we stumbled onto Upper Hutt - described as the dormitory of Wellington - and were put up by Tabitha and Chris. While they went off to school we became aquainted with Upper Hutt (it didn't take long) and NZ TV. With Duane's replacement passport ready and nothing stopping us now we set off to Auckland.

Little to say about the trip. Charged MP3 players more than made up for the lack of a working radio and we made it to Auckland without drama and even found the hotel with no trouble at all (stuff like this never happens).

We gave back the car - a sad day! We wandered around Auckland, replenished our depleted reading stock (i have decided to take on some classical literature - complete and unabridged!) and prepared to cross the date line, travel back in time and take on American customs. Wish us luck!
