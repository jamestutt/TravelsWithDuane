Dion and Duane
16th Feb 2007 - 17th Feb 2007
Czeck me out!

Well Prague. It's a lovely city all in all. They change the guards here like they do in London but they have a band at the window playing a not very honest version of the Tunderbird's theme tune. Also it is always christmas here and never winter - you can buy (and i did) mulled wine all year round and the weather was better than expected (although snow would have been fun). In a word: Quaint. Now this part of Boheimia is also famous for its Beer. And as travellers of the highest caliber we wish to emmerse ourselves in every culture. So having picked up a yank and a ... (i've no idea how to describe someone from Canada), we proceeded to get drunk and put the world to rights (again). And so concludes Prague - I should like to return one day and visit the museum of medival tortue instruments but first to Vienna.
